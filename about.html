Our Story

Five friends, one conviction: that a neighbourhood deserves a wine bar with soul.

Bouteille started the way most good things do — over a bottle of wine and a conversation that went on too long. Five friends, each with their own relationship to wine, their own reasons for caring about it, and a shared frustration: that their neighbourhood didn't have a place that felt right.

Not a wine shop with fluorescent lights and a wall of Bordeaux you can't afford. Not a trendy bar where the staff make you feel stupid for not knowing what pét-nat is. Something in between. Something honest.

"Il faut que les gens se sentent à l'aise et qu'ils soient bien conseillés." Eric Daive

Bouteille is that place. A bar where the wines are chosen with care and priced with honesty. Where the staff know everything and show off nothing. Where the menu changes when the seasons do. Where Monday nights sometimes mean you bring the bottle and we'll handle the rest.

"Comfy, warm light sphere. Relaxed staff, always welcoming, eager to discuss about the new products." François Dangoisse

We're in Stockel because it's home. Place Dumon, the Saturday market, the neighbourhood regulars who become friends — that's what this is about. A wine bar for the people who live here, and for anyone who finds their way through the door.
